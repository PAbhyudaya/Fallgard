Know Us

Dr. ABHISHEK KUMAR

(Founder)

  • Doctorate in Physical Therapy/Rehabilitation from Arizona, USA
  • Postgraduate/master's degree from Sheffield Hallam University (United Kingdom) in Sports Medicine/Injury-PT.
  • Specialized in Orthopedic medicine (ISOM, Ireland) and achieved a Graduated in Bachelor of Physical Therapy (MAHE) and Bachelor of Zoology (Delhi university), a renowned university in India.
  • With nearly two decades of expertise as a Clinical Physical Therapist, Dr. Kumar has built an illustrious career spanning three continents—Asia, Europe, and North America. A graduate of Manipal University, India (BPT), and Delhi University (B.Sc. Zoology), Dr. Kumar further honed his skills through postgraduate education at Sheffield Hallam University, UK, specializing in Sports Medicine, and earned a Doctorate in Physical Therapy from Arizona, USA. He is also certified in Orthopedic Medicine from ISOM, Ireland, and has demonstrated unparalleled dedication to advancing rehabilitation science.

  • Dr. Kumar's innovative spirit is exemplified through his groundbreaking patents. His global-impact invention, "System for Monitoring Users' Posture in Real Time and a Method Thereof," integrates advanced camera, laser, and radar technologies to benefit seniors and individuals with disabilities. He has also filed a provisional U.S. patent for a "Personalized Predictive Fall Detection and Postural Mapping Device using AI," underscoring his commitment to leveraging technology for societal benefit.
  • Currently based in Gainesville, Florida, Dr. Kumar serves as a Clinical Physical Therapist Case Manager and Director of Training and Education. He oversees patient care and staff development while contributing to cutting-edge research in collaboration with scientists worldwide. His extensive clinical experience spans musculoskeletal injuries, sports rehabilitation, geriatrics, pain management, and acute care.
  • Dr. Kumar is a distinguished advisory board member of the University of Alabama's Superfund Research Center, part of an NIH-funded program investigating the impact of heavy metals on lung disease in vulnerable populations. He also serves as Chief Advisor and Board Member for 10CAN, USA, focusing on preventing PTSD and suicides among veterans. Additionally, he consults on health, safety, and rehabilitation initiatives in India, playing a pivotal role in establishing rehabilitation centers.
  • Beyond his professional achievements, Dr. Kumar has consistently demonstrated a deep commitment to community service. He has volunteered with global organizations like Oxfam, Special Olympics Ireland/Europe, and elder care facilities in the U.S., embodying his lifelong passion for societal contribution.
  • A visionary leader in physical therapy and biomedical innovation, Dr. Kumar continues to inspire and make a lasting impact on healthcare, research, and the global community.
