AI Advancements for Fall Prevention in Geriatric Care

Technological innovation is transforming healthcare, enhancing patient care, disease prevention, and research through advancements like artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), wearable devices, telemedicine, and advanced diagnostics. These innovations are driving better health outcomes while making care more accessible and personalized globally.

One such transformative advancement is FallGard, an innovative healthcare device that leverages AI to predict and analyze fall risks in elderly patients, providing real-time monitoring and preventive interventions to improve quality of life and reduce healthcare costs.
